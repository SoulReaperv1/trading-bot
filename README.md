# SOUL REAPER AI Expert Advisor

## Overview
The SOUL REAPER AI Expert Advisor (EA) is an advanced trading bot designed to help users automate their trading strategies in financial markets. This EA utilizes a variety of indicators and parameters to make informed trading decisions, optimizing trading performance while minimizing risks.

## Features
- **Automated Trading**: Trade automatically based on predefined strategies.
- **Customizable Parameters**: Adjust risk, lot size, and other settings to tailor the trading experience.
- **Multiple Indicators**: Supports various technical indicators for better market analysis.
- **Risk Management**: Implements stop loss, take profit levels, and trailing stops.

## Parameters
- `LotSize`: Defines the size of the trades made by the EA.
- `StopLoss`: The maximum loss allowed for a trade before it is closed.
- `TakeProfit`: The profit target where the trade will be closed.
- `TrailingStop`: The ability to lock in profits as the market moves in favor.
- `MaxDrawdown`: The maximum allowable drawdown for the account.
- `Slippage`: The maximum permissible price slippage for orders.

## Indicators Supported
- **Moving Averages**: SMA, EMA for trend detection.
- **Relative Strength Index (RSI)**: For overbought and oversold conditions.
- **Bollinger Bands**: To assess market volatility.
- **MACD**: For momentum analysis.

## Usage Instructions
1. **Installation**: Download and install the SOUL REAPER EA from the repository.
2. **Setup**: Copy the EA file to the appropriate directory in your trading platform (e.g., MetaTrader 4/5).
3. **Configuration**: Open the settings and configure the parameters according to your trading strategy.
4. **Enable Auto Trading**: Ensure that auto trading is enabled in your platform before running the EA.
5. **Monitoring**: Monitor the performance through the designated logs and reports.

## Configuration Details
- Before using the SOUL REAPER EA, please review the configuration settings. Adjust the parameters based on your risk tolerance and trading strategy.
- After configuration, backtest the EA to ensure it meets your expectations in various market conditions.
